Tea Life

In Progress, Mobile App

Tea Life is a mobile app that helps tea lovers discover new teas in a delightful way

inVision interactive prototype (work in progress)

Why Tea Life?

I really enjoy tea, and love finding new teas to try. I couldn't find any Android app that helps users discover, acquire, and steep tea in a delightful way. Most tea app interface will have a menu of tea categories such as "black," "green," "herbal" for users to browse. Tea Life helps users explore new teas through their flavor profiles, and common tea categories.

The end goal is to write a mobile web app that so everyone can find new teas to try!
