Be The Link Web Design
Course

I designed the website for Be The Link, a non-profit organization that assists women in finding jobs. One of the main goals for a non-profit organization is generating donations. I paid special attention to the donation page to create the page to nudge people to donate from the bar graph to encourage people to hit the goal. I used elements from Kickstarter as inspiration.
