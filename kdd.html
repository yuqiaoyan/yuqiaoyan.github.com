KDD 2012 Matching System (  View the system  )
Client
  • KDD Social Board
Role
  • Project manager and back end developer
  • Lead a small team to develop the information retrieval system
  • Negotiated payment, gathered user requirements, and wrote design specification
Results
  • Successfully deployed to 1000+ KDD attendees on August 10, 2012
  • Received positive feedback from users; board plans to reuse our system for 2013

One of my passions is enabling students to grow outside of the classroom setting. The goal of the KDD 2012 Matching system is matching students and data mining experts based on academic interests so they connect during the Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining (KDD) 2012 conference.

One of the unique attributes of our system is using social connections to include into our matching algorithm.
