Information Spread on Twitter
Course
  • Social Network Analysis

News of Muammar Gaddafi's death on 10/20/11 attracted global attention, and generated several terrabytes of tweets. How do we characterize the tweets of Gaddafi over time? Using Twitter's API we listened and filtered for all Gaddafi related tweets. We then visualize retweets as they spread across the network.

We also look at follower count and how effective it is at predicting retweets. While many people may believe that follower count is good for predicting retweets, it is not effective for an accurate prediction alone. We do see that maybe a follower threshold may be required.
