Be The Link

Graphic Design

This is a concept website design for Be The Link, a non-profit organization that assists women in finding jobs. One of the main goals for a non-profit organization is generating donations. Special attention was paid to the donation page to nudge people to donate. The bar graph for example encourages people to help hit the goal. I used elements from Kickstarter as inspiration.
