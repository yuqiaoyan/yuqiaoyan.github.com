Bonnie's Birthday

September 24th, 2013

By Bonnie Yu

Bonnie asked her boyfriend to create a romantic experience that she will never forget for her birthday. It was simple or so she thought. She wanted to have a romantic dinner, watch the star together, and stay up to watch the sunrise.

Her boyfriend rented a guest house at a lovely cottage in front of the lake with a beautiful Lake View, in the San Juan Islands. Unfortunately, the weather was not on her side. It turned out to be a cloudy and foggy day. No stars, and no sunrise :(
